OJT EXPERIENCE

During my 486-hour internship at Pixel8 in the Frontend Development Unit, I gained proficiency in Vue.js, Quasar, Axios, and the JSON server. My experience was really extraordinary, I thought it would be a quiet one wherein I will just do my task assigned to me, but it all turned out differently. I became a team lead only days after I started, and I am very much grateful to be given this opportunity that’s why I gladly accepted it and took this chance because I believe it could help me for my own personal growth, and at the same time midway, I realized that I could also have an impact to others. And I do hope that that impact is a positive one. As a team leader, I improved my communication skills by interacting with diverse colleagues and effectively managing tasks and meetings. I also enhanced my problem-solving abilities by staying ahead of the team, consulting documentation, and encouraging collaboration. Balancing overlapping tasks from my internship and academics taught me prioritization and time management.

Another special experience during my internship was to attend the Leadership Development Program, as not all interns have the chance to join which me grateful for such opportunity. It was quite an eye-opener with the concepts to become an effective leader and a person in general. It was also insightful as even real-life experiences are shared by the supervisors. Upon the completion of my internship, I also did not expect to receive another certificate from the company that commended my dedication throughout my rendered hours. It was truly a remarkable experience which made me reflect as well with what I have done during the internship, as an intern and as a team leader.

This internship simulated a real-world work environment, preparing me to adapt to different professional settings and understand various personalities. The experience at Pixel8 has been instrumental in developing my skills and confidence, bringing me closer to my career goals. Although my internship has ended, I will continue to pursue my goals with the knowledge and experience gained.
